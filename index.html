Welcome! We are Coastalcrest.
Drilling & Engineering Services Company
From Nigeria

Coastalcrest Energy Limited is an indigenous oil & gas services company delivering safe, reliable and efficient drilling and engineering solutions. We specialize in rig leasing, mobilization, maintenance, and technical crew support — combining deep local expertise with global best practices.

With a strong safety culture and decades of combined industry experience, we provide end-to-end support for upstream and pipeline projects, ensuring operations are completed on time, on budget, and to international standards.

Our Contacts
Rig Leasing & Contract Drilling Provision of fully equipped rigs with experienced crews for safe and efficient operations.

Trusted drilling services for upstream projects

We lease modern drilling rigs, backed by skilled operational teams, for both short-term campaigns and long-term contracts. Our rigs meet international standards and are deployed with a focus on safety and reliability.

  • 01. Onshore & nearshore operations
  • 02. Flexible contract terms
  • 03. Skilled drilling crews
  • 04. Safety-first operations
Rig Mobilization & Installation End-to-end rig transport, assembly and commissioning solutions.

Seamless rig movement and setup

Our team handles logistics, transport, and installation of rigs, ensuring safe and timely commissioning at project sites. We work closely with operators to minimize downtime and optimize deployment.

  • 01. Mobilization planning
  • 02. Transportation & logistics
  • 03. Rig assembly & installation
  • 04. Site commissioning
Rig Maintenance & Refurbishment Prolonging rig life with preventive and corrective maintenance.

Keeping rigs safe, efficient and ready

We deliver preventive and corrective maintenance, refurbishments and upgrades that improve rig performance, reduce downtime, and extend operational lifespan.

  • 01. Preventive maintenance
  • 02. Major overhauls
  • 03. Upgrades & retrofits
  • 04. Downtime reduction
Technical Support & Crew Management Experienced drilling personnel and onsite technical guidance.

The right people, every time

We supply skilled drilling crews, manage rotations, and provide onsite technical supervision to ensure smooth and safe operations.

  • 01. Competent personnel supply
  • 02. Crew rotation planning
  • 03. Competency management
  • 04. Onsite technical supervision
Pipeline & Flowline Solutions Engineering and installation for pipeline and flowline systems.

Reliable flowline and pipeline support

We support pipeline installation and integrity operations for both onshore and nearshore projects. Our services include engineering, monitoring, and intervention solutions to maintain flow assurance and safety.

  • 01. Pipeline installation
  • 02. Integrity management
  • 03. Flowline interventions
  • 04. Subsea & onshore support
